About Us

The Philippine Statistics Authority (PSA) is the primary statistical arm of the Philippine Government. We are committed to deliver relevant, reliable, and timely statistics and civil registration services for informed decision-making, sustainable development, and improved quality of life for all Filipinos.

Vision

Global leader of dynamic and transformative statistics, civil registration, and identification services.

Mission

We provide statistics, civil registration, and identification services for sustainable, innovative, and inclusive development.

Core Values

Integrity

We observe at all times the highest standards of ethics, conduct, and professionalism.

Service Excellence

We commit to deliver high-quality, accurate, and timely statistics and civil registration services.

Adaptability

We continuously innovate and adapt to the changing needs of our stakeholders and technology.
